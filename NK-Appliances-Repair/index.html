About Us


We are committed to providing support for both residential and commercial properties, specializing in appliance repairs and air conditioning maintenance, as well as installation and repair services. Our expertise also extends to electrical repairs and rewiring for residential properties across the Western Cape region. We proudly service a wide range of top brands, including AEG, Bosch, Defy, LG, KIC, Samsung, Russell Hobbs, and various other renowned names in the industry.


NK Appliances have experienced technicians for all brands of appliances. You probably thought you would struggle to find technician servicing agents for SMEG and AEG, but you won’t as we are a preferred supplier of parts for them as well as Speed Queen and Bosch. Ask our agents about all the names such as; Defy, Samsung, Whirlpool, and LG including Hisense and Bosch appliance parts and servicing prices. Get it all working again with one phone call to the best in town. We have been trusted with suppliers and customers for more than a decade to deliver super service and prices.


Our Values


MISSION: Our goal is to provide value added service to every customer to become the contractor of choice for contractors and owners needing quality electrical services. A repeat customer relationship through confidence in our service and capabilities is the ultimate goal.


VISION: We aim to lead the industry in providing top-tier solutions for residential and commercial properties. We aim to set new standards in appliance repairs, air conditioning, and electrical services.
